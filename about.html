About Us

The Horror Cinema Museum is dedicated to preserving the rich history of horror films. Our collection spans decades, showcasing the evolution of the genre from its inception to modern-day masterpieces.

We aim to educate and entertain our visitors by providing insights into the filmmaking process, the cultural impact of horror films, and the creative minds behind them.

Join us as we explore the dark and fascinating world of horror cinema!
